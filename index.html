Payment Services Directive2

On November 25. 2015, the European Parliament and Council decided upon directive (EU) 2015/2366, which replaces directive 2007/64/EC of November 13. 2007. PSD2 defines a set of roles allowing unifed access to banking data in the European Union on different kind of restriction levels. PSD2 allows Account Information Service Providers to access transactions, balances and account data for providing read-only services.
